Academic Literacy in Global Contexts

Click to view larger version
Academic literacy empowers students to navigate digital landscapes while maintaining critical engagement with information and communication technologies.

Modern academic literacy extends beyond traditional text-based skills to encompass digital fluency, visual literacy, and the ability to critically evaluate information across multiple platforms and formats. This evolution is essential for students to participate effectively in contemporary academic and professional environments.
